BEFORE YOU UPLOAD

Hack the algorithm like a pro.

A 30-second read that 90% of new creators skip — and then wonder why their reach is dead.

  1. 1

    Train the algorithm first.

    Before posting in your niche, spend 2-3 days liking, following, and saving the kind of videos you're going to make. This teaches TikTok / Instagram / YouTube your niche and your audience — so when you post, the algorithm already knows who to push your video to.

  2. 2

    Post at peak hours.

    Best windows according to most platform algorithms:

    Morning
    8 – 9 AM
    Evening
    5 – 7 PM
  3. 3

    Consistency beats virality.

    One viral video won't pay rent. Five OK videos a week will. Set a posting cadence, stick with it, and let the algorithm do compound-interest math on your behalf.
